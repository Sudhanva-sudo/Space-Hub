About Stars

Stars are the fundamental building blocks of galaxies. These luminous celestial bodies are made up of hot gases, primarily hydrogen and helium, and they produce light and heat through nuclear fusion.

Famous Constellations

Orion

One of the most recognizable constellations, Orion represents a hunter from Greek mythology.

Ursa Major

Known as the Great Bear, Ursa Major is famous for containing the Big Dipper asterism.

Cassiopeia

This W-shaped constellation is named after a vain queen in Greek mythology.
