The Solar System Planets

Mercury

The smallest planet in our solar system and closest to the Sun.

Venus

Known as Earth's twin, Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere and is the hottest planet.

Earth

Our home planet, the only planet known to support life.

Mars

The Red Planet, with signs that water once flowed on its surface.

Jupiter

The largest planet in our solar system, famous for its Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Famous for its stunning ring system, Saturn is the second-largest planet.

Uranus

A gas giant with a unique tilt, spinning on its side.

Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun, known for its intense blue color and storms.
